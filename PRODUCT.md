# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Musicians, sound designers, and curious audio makers who want to turn a short, clean
one-note recording into editable synthesizer parameters. They arrive to understand what
NeuroWave does, see credible results, and decide whether to try the Windows desktop app.

## Product Purpose

NeuroWave listens to a clean one-note clip, predicts an editable synth patch, and renders a
comparison. The website should make that workflow and its limits easy to understand, present
real examples credibly, and lead visitors to the verified Windows release when available.

## Brand Personality

Modern, precise, and quietly high-quality. The site should feel considered and technically
confident without becoming producer-culture styling, cyberpunk, or an over-explained AI
product pitch. Motion should clarify sequence and reward attention, never compete with the
content.

## Anti-references

- No neon, cyberpunk, sci-fi control-room, or glowing-grid aesthetics.
- No generic AI SaaS layout: repeated rounded-card grids, oversized metrics, or empty claims.
- No complicated decorative elements, dense dashboards, or animation that delays reading.
- No unsupported claims about general audio recreation, cloud inference, or model accuracy.

## Design Principles

- Prove the workflow: show the app, the audio comparison, and the editable result before
  making broad claims.
- Precision earns trust: use clear labels, model versions, source notes, and honest limits.
- Let the experience breathe: prefer a few decisive visual moments to a crowded page.
- Motion has a job: use restrained, reduced-motion-safe transitions to explain progression
  and reinforce hierarchy.
- Make the release state obvious: never offer a download until the linked release is verified.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and keyboard-accessibility expectations. Provide visible focus
states, semantic page structure, text alternatives for screenshots/spectrograms, labelled
audio controls, and a reduced-motion experience that preserves all essential information.
